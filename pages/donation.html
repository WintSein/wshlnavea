Help Us Keep NAVEA Park Green!

Your support ensures a beautiful, safe, and clean space for everyone in the community.

Help Us Grow a Better Park

Every contribution, big or small, makes a real difference to the park's future.

All donations are processed securely.

Your Impact

Funds raised go directly to:

  • Tree Planting & Garden Care: New native trees and maintenance of flower beds.
  • Facility Maintenance: Repairing benches, pathways, and playground equipment.
  • Cleanliness & Safety: Providing trash disposal and ensuring security staff presence.
  • Park Programs: Supporting community events and educational initiatives.

What Your Donation Achieves:

5,000 Ks: A pack of new seeds for the Community Garden.

10,000 Ks: One hour of watering and weeding a garden section.

20,000 Ks: Contributes to a major repair of a broken park bench.
