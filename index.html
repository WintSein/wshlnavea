Why NAVEA Matters

NAVEA is designed to connect the community with NAVEA Park, providing easy access to information, updates, and events. By keeping visitors informed, we encourage responsible park usage, increase participation in environmental activities, and help preserve the natural beauty of the park for future generations. From checking weather conditions before a visit to signing up for cleanup events, this website empowers everyone to take part in protecting and enjoying our green spaces.

Park Weather Notes & Safety Tips

Rain Policy

Most trails remain open in light rain. However, the Bamboo Forest Trail and Upper Lagoon Walk may close temporarily during heavy rainfall due to slippery surfaces and flash flood risks.

High Heat/UV Warning

During the hot season (March–May), temperatures can exceed 38°C (100°F). Please carry extra water, wear a hat, and plan strenuous activities for the early morning or late afternoon.

Wind Advisory

If wind speeds exceed 30 mph, the boat rentals will be suspended for visitor safety. Please check with the Visitor Center.
